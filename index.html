Experience

What each role taught me.

Afterpay / Clearpay · Block Inc, NYSE: SQ
CMO / VP Marketing, International
5+ years · AU, NZ, UK and Canada · Delivered double-digit YoY GPV growth for 5 consecutive years

Afterpay's merchant-consumer flywheel put marketing at the heart of the commercial engine. We grew the brand largely by being a genuine growth engine for some of the world's biggest retailers — which in turn grew us. I joined when the business was an ASX darling and stayed through its evolution into part of a US S&P 500 company alongside Square, Cash App, and Tidal.

What made Afterpay unusual was the brand itself — a financial services product that operated like a lifestyle brand, deeply embedded in fashion, culture, and the lives of Gen Z and millennial consumers. That duality shaped how we marketed: commercial rigour on one side, cultural relevance on the other.

I led the brand through multiple growth phases — from disruptor to household name — and built Afterpay Day into a retail sales event that delivered double-digit growth every year. The team were Effies finalists for campaigns that drove measurable commercial ROI across five categories in 2024.

Kayo Sports · News Corp
Head of Marketing
Brand launch from zero · 700k+ subscribers · 83% brand awareness · Sports Marketing Team of the Year

A startup inside a large media company — all the ambition, with the added complexity of a corporate parent. I was part of the founding team: helped name the brand, built the identity from scratch, then led all marketing to scale the service to over 700,000 subscribers and 83% brand awareness within two and a half years.

The work spanned large-scale product-brand campaigns tied to major sports moments, leading a sizable in-house creative team across Kayo, BINGE, and Watch AFL/NRL, and launching eight major campaigns across the period. The team won Mumbrella's Sports Marketing Team of the Year.

Optus
Associate Director, Digital Marketing
~6 years · Ecommerce, performance marketing and digital customer marketing

Where I went deep on performance marketing and ecommerce. I built Optus's first mobile site, led retention and digital customer marketing, and owned ecommerce channel development. This is where I learned to connect brand and commercial outcomes in a highly competitive, price-sensitive category.

Vodafone and 3 Mobile
Various Roles
~9 years combined · Digital merchandising, prepaid product management, retail distribution

My foundations. Hands-on experience across digital merchandising, prepaid segment management, and mass-market retail distribution through Coles and Woolworths. At 3 Mobile I was a product manager, designing the first prepaid product — which gave me a commercial and product lens I've carried through everything since.
